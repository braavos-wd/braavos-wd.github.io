  • Tax Reimbursements.
    • When you buy you are taxed 15%. But effective tax is 8% as 7% will be returned to you in tokens "during your sell transaction". Instead of tokens sitting idle in your wallet, the contract borrows them, and uses them for more incentives.
  • Assign rewards to charity.
    • You can assign a specific wallet to claim your reflections. Give a stream of income to your sibling, church, or charity. One wallet can have one beneficiary only, but assigned wallet can also be beneficiary of many wallets.
  • Reflections Poaching - Sealed Bid Auctions
    • Tokens loaned to contract (reimbursement tokens) accrue reflections, which are auctioned, winning bid decides where they go, between community or charity. Creates wiggle room to host occasional charitable events.
  • Temple Challenge - Configure Sell Tax & Reimbursements
    • This gamefi challenge configures your sell tax & reimbusement position. losing does not spell doom. winnings odds are constant with limited play chances each day, win and your sell tax is 8% (or close to half).
  • Lucky dip into the rewards pool
    • You could win 2% of the rewards pool, by simply buying or taking the challenge. So check your wallet after each buy. Our buy competitions are not about the biggest amount, any amount works, its just chance.
  • Stake to claim reimburse tokens rewards.
    • To claim reflections from all your reimbursement tokens, stake an amount equal or great to your reimbursement tokens, for 30 Days. Rewards from your reimbursement tokens can be switched by the admin between Auction & Stakers.
  • Last man standing reward incentive.
    • Its in how we calculate your share of reflections.
    Normal reflections calc:
    eth_reward_pool * wallet_tokens/total_supply.

    Braavos reflections calc:
    eth_reward_pool * wallet_tokens/(total_supply - burnt_tokens - dex_tokens).

    taxes explained



    Buy Tax: 15% { net 8%, 7% reimbursed in tokens during your sell tx }

    Sell Tax: 15% { configurable to 8% }

    Net Effective Tax = 16% {8% Buy + 8% Sell. 14% out of 30% is reverted through using the project utility}

    ----------------------

    Tax Revenue Usage:

    All taxes are liquidated to eth

    1/3 buy backs, 1/3 reflections, 1/3 farming.
